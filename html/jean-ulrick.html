Ulrick M. Jean

Artist from Haiti ( Late 1960s - )

  • Ulrick M. Jean was born in the late 1960s in Carrefour, Port-au-Prince. He originally sold his work through various vendors at the Iron Market in Port-au-Prince. Today his work is represented in the United States by The Haitian Art Company in Key West, Florida.

  • An example of his work can be found in Jonathan Demme's Island on Fire.
