The Internet Art Exchange

Featuring an Online Gallery of Haitian Art

The Internet Art Exchange is proud to offer this curated selection of Haitian Art. Every painting offered for sale here currently hangs in the home of a collector of Haitian Art. Each painting was deliberately chosen, with a primary consideration being a desire to live with the piece, to revisit and reconsider it almost every day.

Unfortunately, wall space in our homes is limited. That's where selling from our collections comes in: While each sale forces us to part with a painting we love, it also offers us the opportunity to adopt another.

  • We're happy to answer questions and provide additional images.
  • We pack well and ship promptly upon clearance of payment.
  • Satisfaction guaranteed with returns accepted for any reason.

Browse the gallery by selecting from the Artist Index on the left. You will find multiple images of each painting offered for sale. The second image of any work is always of an even higher resolution than the first, allowing you to zoom in on every detail. Next to the price of each painting, you will find a link that will allow you to directly email the owner/seller to ask questions or arrange a purchase.

Enjoy!
