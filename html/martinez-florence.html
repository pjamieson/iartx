Florence Martinez

Artist from Haiti ( 25 April 1942 - before 1997 )

  • Florence Martinez was born April 25, 1942 in Jacmel. He attended school in Port-au-Prince, and joined the Centre d'Art in 1959. Early in his career he won a painting competition held by the Dames du Corps Diplomatique, and was one of the last painters to add a mural to the walls of the Episcopal Cathedral Sainte Trinite in Port-au-Prince. His very unusual, almost studied, style is unlike any other.

  • Now deceased, Martinez's work is included in the permanent collections of the Musee d'Art Haitien du College Saint Pierre in Port-au-Prince, and the Davenport Museum of Art in Iowa.

[Biographic notes from: Demme, Jonathan. Island on Fire. New York: Kaliko Press, 1997.]
