Eddy Pierre

Artist from Haiti ( Mid 1960s - )

  • According to Boris Kravitz, owner of the Haitian Art Company, Eddy Pierre was born in the mid-1960s and lives in Cap-Haitien. He is a cousin of the artist Michelle Obin.

  • Often, Eddy Pierre's paintings show Haitian market women carrying laden baskets on their heads. He usually shows them as over-sized compared to the houses and other objects in the painting. This symbolizes the importance of these women to Haitian society.
