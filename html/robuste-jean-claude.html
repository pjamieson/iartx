Jean Claude Robuste

Artist from Haiti ( 24 Oct 1946 - )

  • Jean Claude Robuste was born in Port-au-Prince on October 24, 1946. His father was in the military and his mother was a dress designer. He attended Ecole Nationale des Casernes Dessalines and Lycee Francois Duvalier. In 1969 he began drawing and painting with David Rouzier and Frank Robuste, his older brother.
