Kingsley Thomas

Artist from Jamaica ( 1941 - )

  • Kingsley Thomas was born in Jamaica in 1941. His subject matter differs from the other Jamaican Intuitive Artists. He creates imagined dramatic scenes and sometimes paints a caption onto his paintings. Thomas's art is often a medium for messages and moralizing.
