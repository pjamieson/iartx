Fritzner Obin

Artist from Haiti ( 1967 - )

  • Fritzner Obin was born in 1967 in Port-au-Prince, Haiti. He is the father of two daughters and a son. Originally inspired by the artist Voltaire Hector, he has been painting since the mid-1990's. His paintings usually depict Haitian daily life or vodou scenes. His work can be described as creative, whimsical, humorous, and impulsive.

  • In addition to being a painter, Fritzner Obin writes for a local newspaper, and is involved with the radio production 'Radio Magic in Magic.'
