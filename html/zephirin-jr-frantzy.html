Frantz "Frantzy" Zephirin Jr

Artist from Haiti ( 1989 - 08 Dec 2008 )

  • Frantz "Frantzy" Zephirin Jr was the son of the artist Frantz Zephirin. A talented artist in his own right, Frantzy's promising career was cut short when he died at the age of 19 due to complications from diabetes.
