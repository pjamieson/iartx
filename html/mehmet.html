Mehmet Ali Resimcioğlu

Artist from Turkey ( 1930 - 1983 )

  • Mehmet Ali Resimcioğlu was born in 1930 in Izmir, Turkey. He studied at the Art Institute of Izmir from 1946 to 1950. After going to Germany in 1965, he had his first solo exhibition in Wiesbaden in 1974. Later returning to Turkey, he had exhibitions in Instanbul and Ankara, and came to be recognized as a Turkish Master Artist.

  • Resimcioğlu died in 1983 in Istanbul.
